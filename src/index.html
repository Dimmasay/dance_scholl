ABOUT US
Everything in the universe has a rhythm. Everything dances." (Maya Angelou)
RHYTHM dance school is a school of modern and classical dance for children. The school's program is based on the principle of a dance academy and involves teaching modern choreography through mastering gymnastics, classical dance, developing technique, developing plasticity, flexibility, the ability to improvise and their own pieces of choreography.
Children work in the directions of contemporary, modern jazz dance and participate in concert and festival activities, learning the history of dance as well as principles and methods of improvisation.
The effectiveness of the classes is achieved through the unique method of teaching modern choreography.
The main goal of the school is to spark children's love for dance through the feeling of themselves and their bodies in space, as well as to help them take their first steps in the wonderful profession of a dancer.
